PRIVACY POLICY HIGHLIGHTS
--------------------------
- Personal information collected includes name, email, address, profile data
- Data collected through account creation, contact requests, location services, purchases, cookies
- Data used to provide services, authenticate users, respond to inquiries, process transactions
- Information shared with event organizers, analytics providers (Google Analytics), payment processors
- Credit card details handled exclusively by PCI-DSS certified third parties
- Users can unsubscribe from emails, access/delete personal data, disable location sharing
- GDPR compliant with data deletion and portability rights
- Data retained during service use; after termination, kept for 90 days minimum for legal compliance
- Tickets99 acts as data processor on behalf of event organizers
